The Florida Collection provides a comprehensive archive of digitized materials related to Florida's history, with a particular focus on Northeast Florida and Jacksonville. Explore a wide range of resources, from digitized photographs of Jacksonvilleâ€™s neighborhoods to captivating postcards.

African American History Digital Community

The African American History Digital Community Archive is a curated local history collection of original source material related to African American life in Northeast Florida. The collection includes photographs, personal papers, scrapbooks, yearbooks, pamphlets, and gray literature related to civic organizations or commercial enterprises. In the Digital Community Archive, the Library provides access to a collection of unique original source material from a variety of local resources, including community members and partner organizations, that would be otherwise unavailable to the general public.

Genealogy

The Genealogy Collection offers a wealth of digitized resources to assist in tracing your Florida family history, particularly those with roots in Jacksonville. Explore city directories, digitized yearbooks from local high schools, and historical cemetery records to uncover valuable details about your ancestors.
