Florida Collection

About this collection

The Florida Collection is a digital archive that provides comprehensive access to digitized materials chronicling Florida's history, with a particular focus on Northeast Florida and Jacksonville. The collection encompasses a wide range of resources, including photographs, newspapers, postcards, and archival materials. Users can explore topics such as Jacksonville's historic neighborhoods, the Civil War era, and the Great Fire of 1901. Additionally, the collection offers unique glimpses into Jacksonville's past through personal papers, rare books, and oral histories.
